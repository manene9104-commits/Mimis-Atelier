Welcome to Mimi's Atelier

Rooted in the rich cultural landscapes of Anambra, Nigeria, Mimi's Atelier merges traditional Igbo aesthetics, intricate local beadwork, and clean contemporary silhouettes. From breathtaking custom Asoebi designs to majestic modern corporate and casual wears, we dress you to make bold assertions of elegance.
